A guide for your disagreements

Start Solving a Problem

Disagreements persist because solving them requires you to alternate between two different modes of thinking, often in coordination with others.

Getting out of sync while solving issues together can lead to offending or misleading your peers without you even noticing. Small miscommunications lead to big issues!

Solve Guide coordinates group issue solving by guiding you when to Think Wide, and when to Think Narrow.

Thinking Wide

is creative — imagining all possibilities without judgment.

Thinking Narrow

is critical — choosing the best available path forward.

You will solve more issues for good if you can observe reality creatively and navigate it critically.
