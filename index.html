Your issues persist because solving them requires you to alternate between two different modes of thinking, often in coordination with others.

Getting out of sync while solving issues together can lead to offending or misleading your peers without you even noticing. Small miscommunications lead to big issues!

Solve Guide coordinates group issue solving. For now you can create an account and solve issues solo. Multiplayer mode is coming!

The two modes of thinking are:

Widening is creative; imagine all the possibilities without judgement.

Narrowing is critical; choose the best available path forward.

You will solve more issues for good if you can observe reality creatively and navigate it critically.
